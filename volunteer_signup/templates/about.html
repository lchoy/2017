About

What is the purpose of this site?

The site is for the Key Club to use for signing up volunteers.
The way this works is that event coordinators will create events.
Administrators can send emails about upcoming events.
People who want to sign up for events can volunteer for them.

Who Is Behind All This Wonderful Code?

The Wellesley 2017-2018 Girls Who Code have worked very hard to make this site for you!

Golden Narwhals In The Making

What is Girls Who Code?

Girls Who Code is a national non-profit organization leading the movement to inspire, educate,
and equip girls with the computing skills to pursue 21st century opportunities.
The coded this with the help of three adults.
