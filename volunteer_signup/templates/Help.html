Help

1. How do I create an account?


First click the "sign up" tab on the menu bar,
then insert your information to create an account!
You will then be able to sign in.

2. How do I add an event?

Once you have logged in, click on the "events"
tab on the menu bar, and click "add event" in
the bottom left corner. Next, put in all the
required information for your event, and click submit!


3. How do I sign up for an event?

Once you are logged in, go to the "event" page,
and click "sign up" on the event you would
like to volunteer for.


4. How do I change my account information?

After you are signed in, click "profile" on the
menu bar. Then click "update profile" under
your information. Click "submit" and your
profile will be updated!!
