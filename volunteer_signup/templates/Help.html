Help

1. How do I create an account?

First click the "login" tab on the menu bar,
then click the words "sign up" near the bottom of the login boxes.
Insert your information to create an account!
You will then be able to login in with the information you put in.


2. How do I add an event?

Once you have logged in, click on the "events"
tab on the menu bar, and click "add event" in
the bottom left corner. Next, put in all the
required information for your event, and click submit!


3. How do I sign up for an event?

Once you are logged in, go to the "event" page,
and click "sign up" on the event you would
like to volunteer for.


4. How do I change my account information?

After you are signed in, click "profile" on the
menu bar. Then click "update profile" under
your information. Fill in your new info in the boxes.
Click "submit" and your
profile will be updated!!


5. How do I delete my account?

Once you've signed in, click "profile" on the menu bar.
At the bottom of the page, there will be a button that says "Delete Your Account".
Press the button and then press OK,
and you will have successfully deleted your account.
